About the System

The Citizen Grievance Management System is designed to provide citizens with a transparent and efficient platform for raising, tracking, and resolving grievances related to society and community services.

Features Available

  • Register and manage citizen accounts
  • Raise complaints and grievances
  • Track complaint status in real time
  • View complaint history
  • Update profile information
  • Change account password securely
  • Receive updates on complaint progress
